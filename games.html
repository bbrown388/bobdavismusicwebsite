Games

Each one built in a single AI session. Rough, but real. New ones keep showing up.

Game 38
Dust Road Derby
Five laps around a dirt oval against three AI rivals with rubber-band difficulty. Hold left or right to steer your line. Tuck behind a rival to trigger slipstream -- an 18% speed boost with a wind-trail visual. First real-time racing game in the series: parametric oval track, positional scoring, dust-cloud physics.
Game 37
Tumbledown Town
Tap the glowing supports to knock them out, one at a time. Guide the collapse to crush the wanted outlaws beneath the rubble -- but the innocents have to walk away. First real physics demolition puzzle in the series: Verlet constraints, chain reactions, five increasingly devious levels.
Game 36
Rodeo Queen
Three events, one crowd energy meter, and a multiplier that climbs every time you nail it. Tap the barrels in rhythm, spin and throw a lasso, then hold and release with the horse's bounce. Chain perfects to push the multiplier to 5x -- miss once and it drops back to one.
Game 35
Snake Canyon
Navigate a torch-lit canyon maze with nothing but your ears to guide you. A rattlesnake lurks in the dark -- its rattle grows louder and shifts left or right as it draws near. Move slow and deliberate. Rush, and it hears you. Reach the exit before the darkness wins.
Game 34
Dust Devil Dance
Draw paths across the desert basin and watch dust devils follow them in loops, each one playing a note from a pentatonic scale. When paths cross, the music resonates. Landmarks warp the sound -- the mesa deepens a devil's voice, the waterhole makes it wobble. The storm is coming. Compose before it arrives.
Game 33
Cattle Auction
You're the auctioneer. Six buyers with distinct budgets and personalities bid on 8 lots of cattle in real time. Watch the room, let the price climb, then tap to call it -- going once, going twice, sold. Wait too long and interest dries up. Call too soon and leave money on the table.
Game 32
Barbed Wire
String barbed wire between fence posts to slow and kill rustlers before they reach your cattle. Tap two adjacent posts to connect them -- wires drain rustler HP but take damage over time and break. Five waves of escalating threats. First tower-defense structure in the series.
Game 31
Pony Express
Ride the rhythm across five terrains from trot to full gallop. Tap when the beat hits the circle -- stay in sync to ride fast, miss the beat and your horse slows. Five escalating sections with increasing BPM and syncopated patterns. First action-rhythm hybrid in the series.
Game 30
Tumbleweed Pinball
Physics pinball on a frontier saloon table. Tumbleweeds roll, cactus bumpers fire back, and three WANTED poster targets unlock a big bounty bonus. Tap left or right to flip -- chain bumper hits for multiplied scores. Full ball physics with gravity, spin, and drain.
Game 29
Jail Break
Turn-based stealth escape from a frontier jail. Pick locks, throw stones to distract guards, slip through shadow zones, and reach the horse outside. Guards patrol with vision cones -- step into the red and it's over. Five escalating layouts.
Game 28
Brand Iron
Drag a glowing brand iron along five cattle-hide templates. Keep the iron in the sweet zone -- too cold leaves no mark, too hot and you burn the hide. Five brands, rising complexity, one shot at the path before the iron cools.
Game 27
River Run
White-water canyon rapids. Steer your raft left and right with momentum -- not lanes. Read the current swirls on the water to predict what's coming, and hold the brake zone to slow down when the channel gets tight. Five rounds, three lives.
Game 26
Dust Storm
A wall of dust has swallowed the road. Your lantern cuts just enough light to see what's coming -- barely. Tap left or right to swerve the stagecoach around boulders that appear out of nowhere. Five rounds, three lives, zero mercy.
Game 25
Fence Line
A storm tore through the fence and the cattle are heading for the gaps. Tap to set posts, tap again to nail the rail -- but the wind keeps knocking your work loose. Close every gap before dawn.
Game 24
Dead Reckoning
Navigate a night cattle drive by the stars. Tap two constellations to draw bearing lines, locate yourself on the terrain map, then guide your herd to the ranch across 5 escalating rounds.
Game 23
Devil's Backbone
Haul a load of dynamite through a mountain pass on a tilting wagon. Hold left or right to counterbalance wind gusts and trail bumps across 5 escalating legs.
Game 22
Gallows Road
Push crates to block the guards, collect keys, unlock doors, and escape the jail. A Sokoban-style puzzle across 5 escalating levels.
Game 21
Wanted: Dead or Alive
Track a fugitive through a frontier town. Interview witnesses, examine evidence, build your confidence -- then make the accusation before time runs out.
Game 20
Trail Boss
Lead a wagon train across 5 biomes. Keep water, food, and axle from running dry. Handle ambushes, river crossings, and supply caches before time runs out.
Game 19
Canyon Crossfire
Three outlaws. One boulder. Watch their reload bars, time your peek, fire fast. Stay exposed too long and they'll put one in you.
Game 18
Dead Man's Hand
Five hands of Texas Hold'em against Snake Pete, Diamondback, and Preacher Jack. Watch their tells, manage your chip stack, survive the showdown.
Game 17
Rope Trick
Five throws, one shot each. Watch the wind arrow and compensate -- lasso the spinning target before the arc drifts past it.
Game 16
Moonshine Run
Keep your outlaw still running through the night. Manage heat, pressure, and proof while revenue agents sweep the dark with lanterns. Hide your glow -- or get busted.
Game 15
Prairie Fire
Wind-driven wildfire is heading for your homestead. Place firebreaks and water buckets with limited resources. Watch the wind shift and protect what's yours.
Game 14
Midnight Rodeo
Ride a bucking bull under the arena lights. Tap left or right to counter his spin. Build crowd energy for a higher score multiplier before you're thrown.
Game 13
Snake Oil
Run Dr. Davis's medicine wagon. Match two ingredients to each customer's ailment before their patience runs out.
Game 12
Stampede
First-person buffalo herd. Dodge left or right. Signal the herd to scatter. Survive as long as you can.
Game 11
Boot Hill Bluff
Read the outlaw's tell, bet your chips, call the bluff or fold.
Game 10
Gold Rush

Game 09

Game 08
