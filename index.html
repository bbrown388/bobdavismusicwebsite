Bob Davis

Born and raised in Iowa, Bob Davis found his home in Texas in 2017 and never looked back. He writes honest, road-worn country music that Nashville forgot how to make. After a 13-year career in software, he traded the keyboard for a different kind of instrument. A 2026 Texas State Songwriters Competition entrant, his debut arrives in early 2027.
